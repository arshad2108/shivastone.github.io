About Us...

We M/S Shiva Stone started processing Industrial Mineral in 2019 in Patratu, Ramgarh, Jharkhand. Since then, we have grown into a diverse and respected enterprise. We have set new benchmarks in the industry with our high quality products and commitment to customer satisfaction.

We are a name that reflects commitment to quality. We diligently follow the policy of customer satisfaction. In order to retain their confidence, we deliver the raw materials as per their requirements.
